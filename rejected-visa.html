To ensure a smooth and successful application process, we have compiled a list of required documents and important information for your reference.

 

here is the comprehensive document list for your visa application for [Country name]:

 

 

Passport Requirements:

  • Must be valid for at least three months after the intended departure from Schengen.
  • Must have at least two blank pages.
  • Must have been issued within the past 10 years.
  • Must not have any observations regarding the front data page.
  • Previous passport(s) (if any) should be included.

 

Passport Copy:

  • All applications must contain a full-color photocopy of the valid passport - all pages of the passport are required.
  • If there are any valid visas for Schengen or USA/UK/Canada/Australia (issued in the last three years) in the old/canceled passport, then a color photocopy of the visa pages and passport data pages is required.

 

Application Form:

  • Fully completed application form.
  • Any incomplete information or false statements may result in a delay in processing.

 

Photograph:

  • One recent, original passport-size color photograph with a white background.
  • Photograph should not be older than six months.

 

Travel Itinerary:

  • Itinerary covering the entire trip in Iceland and all Schengen Member States.
  • Stating the planned dates and places of stay.
  • Evidence of accommodation (hotel reservations) for the entire trip in the Schengen area.
  • Including the names of hotels and their contact information, as well as the duration of stay.

 

Covering Letter:

  • From the applicant introducing themselves and explaining the purpose, duration, and itinerary of the visit.

 

Travel Medical Insurance:

  • Must cover possible expenses in connection with a return for health reasons or death, indispensable medical treatment, or acute hospitalization during the stay.
  • Must cover all Schengen countries.
  • Minimum policy coverage is Euro 30,000 or US Dollar 50,000.
  • Must be valid for the entire duration of the intended stay.
  • The validity of the visa may be shortened if the insurance policy does not cover the entire duration of the stay.

 

Proof of Means of Transport:

  • Flight reservation for return or round ticket.
  • If traveling to several Schengen States, proof of intra-Schengen flight reservation, train itinerary, or car rental.
  • Tickets do not need to be paid, only booking needs to be submitted.

 

Proof of Lodging:

  • Hotel reservations, rental of holiday home, or campus residence reservation.
  • If staying with a family member or friend, proof of sponsorship and/or private accommodation from the host.
  • Individual Schengen States may require proof of sponsorship and/or private accommodation by completing a form provided by the Schengen State's competent authority.
  • If traveling to several Schengen States, proof of lodging in each location should be submitted.

 

Proof of Financial Means:

  • Original bank statement showing movements in the last three months, stamped and signed by the bank.
  • The account should be in the name of the applicant or the sponsor of the trip.
  • If the sponsor is providing financial support, the bank statement should be accompanied by a signed sponsorship letter and a copy of the sponsor's passport.
  • Indian income tax return acknowledgment for the last two assessment years.

 

Documents based on occupation and/or age:

  • If the applicant is employed:
    • Payslips for the last three months.
    • Employment contract.
    • Employer's statement on approval for holidays.
    • If the applicant is a company owner or self-employed:
    • Certificate of registration of the company.
  • If the applicant is retired:
    • Pension statements for the last three months.
    • Proof of regular income generated by ownership of property or business.

 

Minor Applicant:

  • If the minor is traveling with only one parent, written consent of the other parent or guardian, and a signed copy of their passport or other photo ID. In case a person has sole custody or guardianship of the minor, proof of this should be submitted.
  • If the minor travels alone (without parents), written consent from both parents or legal guardians of the minor, as well as signed copies of the passports or other photo IDs of the parents or legal guardians.
  • All minors must be accompanied by both parents or legal guardians and sign the application form in front of a VFS officer. Except in cases where the child is traveling with one parent to visit the other parent, who is residing in Iceland.

 

Additional Document List:

  • A cover letter from the Indian organization or company.
    • Containing the following details: the applicant's identity, purpose of journey, and the period and place of intended stay.
  • Signed Invitation:
    • Containing contact details of the inviting party along with the following details: the applicant's identity, purpose of journey, and the period and place of intended stay.
